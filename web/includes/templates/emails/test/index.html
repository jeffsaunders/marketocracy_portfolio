First Things First

The best way to take advantage of what the Marketocracy platform has to offer is to jump in and start your own funds and practice making real investment decisions.

How does our site work? It's simple.

  • Set up a model fund which has $1 million virtual dollars deposited in it.
  • Purchase stocks for the portfolio using the Trade Wizard.. It's as easy as buying stocks at your online brokerage.
  • Marketocracy then "fills" your orders based on what is actually happening in the market. That means your order won't fill until the next time it is actually traded on the real market. And, if it's a small volume stock, it won't fill unless the volume on the actual market is sufficient.
