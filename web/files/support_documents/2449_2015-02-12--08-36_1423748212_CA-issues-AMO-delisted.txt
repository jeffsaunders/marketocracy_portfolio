At maturity the holder will receive a payment based on the percentage change in the MSCI EAFE Index (Bloomberg index symbol MXEA), starting value 1,141.11 as of 11/26/2008. At the maturity date, if the Index has increased the maturity payment will be based on triple the percentage increase in the index, up to a maximum total payment of $14.14 per note.
